Burger Recipe

Description:

Burgers are made by combining bread buns, ground beef, and any vegetables that the person would like.

Ingredients:

Bread, ground beef, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions, salt, black pepper, ketchup and or mayo.

Steps

  1. Peel onion skin.
  2. Wash all vegetables.
  3. Slice tomatoes into circles.
  4. Slice lettuce into squares.
  5. Season ground beef.
  6. Shape out multiple patties.
  7. Fry up the patties.
  8. Toast the bread buns.
  9. Put lettuce on bottom bread bun.
  10. Put cooked patty on lettuce.
  11. Put cheese on burger
  12. Put sauces like ketchup on burger.
  13. Put tomato on burger.
  14. Put onion on burger.
  15. Put top bun on burger. Done.

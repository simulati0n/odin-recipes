Pancake Recipe

Description:

In order to make pancakes, you need to whisk flour with eggs and water. Then you cook the pancake batter in a... pan.

Ingredients:

Pancake flour, eggs, water, milk

Steps:

  1. Add 2 cups of pancake flour to a bowl.
  2. Add one cup of water.
  3. Add 2 eggs.
  4. Whisk the mixture until the contents are completely mixed with no clumps.
  5. Add more water or flour to adjust the consistency. It should not too thick or liquid, where the mixture drips slowly from the spoon.
  6. Heat up a pan with oil or butter in it.
  7. Add batter to pan in a circular shape.
  8. Flip pancake after a few minutes, make sure each side is golden brown.
  9. You can plate it with fruit or butter on top of it. Done.
